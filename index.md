!!!success :icon-people: Built by the Community
This handbook is put together by member volunteers of the Connectivity Standards Alliance. It's always being updated and improved by the people building the Matter ecosystem. If anything here conflicts with the Matter specification, the SDK, or Alliance policy, those formal documents are the **source of truth**.

We try to keep as much information here in the open as possible. Some links, however, point to resources exclusively available to Alliance members. By becoming a member, you'll gain access to everything and can work with us directly.
